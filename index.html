I was born and raised in South Baltimore, Maryland. I attended Baltimore School for the Arts for high school, which was the catalyst for my love of storytelling. With the speaking and interpersonal skills I gained through the Baltimore School for the Arts, I decided to pursue a new method of storytelling: journalism. My ultimate goal is to become an international broadcast reporter who tells the stories of people around the world. I also hope to run a food blog that reviews all of the dishes I try in my travels.
